Timothy is a policy staffer on Capitol Hill currently working in Majority Whip James E. Clyburn's office. He has experience working on a diverse portfolio that includes government oversight and reform, technology, infrastructure, and student loans.

Prior to coming to D.C., Timothy taught 8th and 9th grade history at a public charter school in Massachusetts. He also served as the teacher representative, working on federal, state, and local policies that impact schools.

Timothy graduated in 2019 with a Master of Public Affairs degree from the LBJ School of Public Affairs at UT Austin and in 2014 with a B.A. in History and Education at Fitchburg State University.
